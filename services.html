Logistics

We assist various types of organizations across Africa plan, implement and execute the movement of goods from point of collection to desired destination in the wholesale and retail industry, automobile and electronics and manufacturing sector. Our services include:
  • Inbound Logistics: The movement, storage and transportation of various components required for production inputs such as raw materials, equipment for manufacturing.
  • Outbound Logistics: The transportation of finished products or any other items from the production facilities to the next supply chain link, point of consumption/ end user.
  • Reverse Logistics: Popular within the automobile and electronics sector. This is the movement of goods or items from end user to the concerned party. This applies to products or items that need to be repaired, recycled or serviced.
